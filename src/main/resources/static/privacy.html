Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal and transaction-related information. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you make a payment on our website, we collect only minimal information required for service delivery, such as:

  • Supporter name (if provided)
  • Transaction ID
  • Payment status

We do not collect or store sensitive payment information such as card numbers, UPI IDs, CVV, PINs, or banking passwords.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used only for:

  • Displaying your name on the public supporter wall
  • Assigning your supporter rank
  • Generating a thank-you message
  • Verifying successful transactions
  • Improving platform performance

We do not use your information for spam, advertising, or unauthorised communication.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal or transaction data with any third party, except where required by law.

4. Third-Party Payment Gateway

All payments are securely processed through Razorpay. Their handling of payment-related data is governed by their own Privacy Policy and security practices.

5. Data Security

We follow appropriate technical and administrative measures to protect stored data from unauthorised access, misuse, or disclosure.

6. User Consent

By using this website and making a payment, you consent to the terms stated in this Privacy Policy.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the website signifies acceptance of the updated policy.
