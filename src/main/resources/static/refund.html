Refund & Cancellation Policy

This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains how refunds are handled for payments made on this platform.

1. Nature of Service

Payments made on this platform are for a digital recognition service where supporter details are displayed instantly after successful payment. Since the service is delivered immediately, standard cancellations are not applicable.

2. No Cancellation

Once a payment is successfully completed through the payment gateway, it cannot be cancelled by the user.

3. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are considered only under the following conditions:

  • Duplicate payment made by the same user
  • Technical failure where the amount is debited but the supporter name and rank are not displayed
  • Payment processing errors caused by system issues

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, users must contact us with valid transaction details such as:

  • Transaction ID
  • Date and time of payment
  • Registered contact details

5. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • Incorrect supporter name entered by the user
  • Change of mind after successful display
  • Successful transactions where the service has already been delivered
